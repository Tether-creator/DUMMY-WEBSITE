Distribution Model

OTOKO’s distribution is designed to incentivize genuine community participation and ensure equitable token allocation:

  • Airdrop: 10,000 OTK per eligible participant after at least three trades.
  • Reserve & Ecosystem: A portion reserved for future ecosystem development and partnerships.
  • Team & Advisors: Tokens allocated to the team (subject to a vesting schedule) to ensure long-term project commitment.
  • Liquidity Provision: Strategically allocated for maintaining healthy market liquidity.

Utility and Use Cases

OTOKO is not just a token—it’s a dynamic asset with multiple utilities:

  • Governance: Empowering token holders to actively vote on major platform decisions.
  • Staking & Rewards: Incentivizing long-term holding through rewarding staking mechanisms.
  • Transactional Efficiency: Low fees and fast transaction times on Binance Smart Chain.
  • Ecosystem Integration: Facilitation of diverse use cases in the evolving DeFi landscape.
