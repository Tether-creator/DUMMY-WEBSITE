Airdrop Details

The OTOKO airdrop is designed to reward genuine community participation and promote long-term holding.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Participants must have completed at least 3 trades on the network.
  • This requirement helps prevent automated bots and ensures active engagement.

Airdrop Amount

Eligible participants receive a fixed allocation of 10,000 OTK tokens. This distribution model is aimed at rewarding engagement while ensuring a fair and transparent allocation process.

Distribution Process

Once eligibility is confirmed on-chain, tokens are automatically transferred to user wallets. All transactions are fully transparent and can be verified on BscScan.

Future Incentives

Beyond the initial airdrop, further incentive programs may be introduced as part of our ongoing commitment to community engagement and project sustainability.
